Coalizer Base Viz (Hard Coded German Bundestag Wahl-O-Mat 2017)

  • Parameters
    The a-Office axis represents the percent of seats in parliment each coalition would control
    The B-Policy axis is a scaled score to represent how much party centric policy the coaltion would be able to implement
    The Office and Policy Axis are proportional and a-Office = 1 - B-Policy
  • Blocks
    A white block represents a non untility maximizing coalition or the opposition
    Opposition
    A shaded block represent a utility maximizing coalition (CS: CDU/CSU&SPD)
    CS
  • Dotted Line
    The dotted lined that runs through the blocks is the utility value for the coalition at the specific office and policy value
    SFL
  • Interpretation In a block, a Minimum Winning Coalition has a range of policy and office control that can be negotiated between coalition members. The range is shown by vertical boundaries of the block and the utility values for the party in that row are depicted by the dotted lines for this range.
